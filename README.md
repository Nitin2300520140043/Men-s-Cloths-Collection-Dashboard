# Men's Collection Dashboard | Insight BI

## Project Overview
The **Men's Collection Power BI Dashboard** provides a comprehensive analysis of men’s apparel brands, focusing on sales performance, profit trends, product variety, and discount strategies.  
This dashboard empowers retail and fashion business teams to monitor brand performance, optimize pricing strategies, and identify top and underperforming brands.

---

## Objective
To deliver data-driven insights into:
- Brand-wise sales and profitability
- Discount and pricing trends
- Product variety and assortment
- Identification of high and low performing brands

---

## Key Insights

### **1. Top 5 Brands by Average Discount %**
- Highest average discounts offered by:
  - iVOC - 89%
  - WUXI – 85%
  - MISCHIEF MONKEY - 85%
  - Voroxy X AG - 83%
  - RED TAPE - 83%
- **Average Discount:** 82.90%

### **2. Top 5 Brands by Highest Average Sales Price**
- Leading brands by average sales price:
  - Armani Exchange - 6.1K
  - Brooks Brothers - 5.1K
  - Terra Luna - 5.0K
  - Scotch & Soda - 4.5K
  - Kingdom of White - 3.6K
- **Average Sales Price:** Rs 3.64K

### **3. Top 5 Brands by Highest Number of Varieties**
- Most diverse product range offered by:
  - The Indian Garage Co - 51 products (22.87%)
  - U.S. Polo Assn. - 44 products (19.73%)
  - SNITCH - 36 products (16.14%)
  - NETPLAY - 38 products (17.04%)
  - THE BEAR HOUSE - 27 products (12.11%)

### **4. Top 5 Brands by Average Profit %**
- Highest profit margins recorded for:
  - American Eagle - 17%
  - Pactimo - 17%
  - SiiLSoul - 17%
  - Be Active X AG - 15%
  - LP JEANS - 15%

### **5. Bottom 5 Brands by Average Profit %**
- Lowest profit margins:
  - AAPSKA - 3%
  - Colorplus - 3%
  - Crown Threads - 3%
  - Lonexo - 3%
  - Maneur - 3%
